Lab News

We are very excited, and sad, to say goodbye to two of our excellent and hard-working undergraduate reseatchers, Julia Cline and Kit Lafevre.

Julia spent a good part of the last two years working on her research looking at cryoprotective properties of carbohydrates in temperate forest ants.

Kit spent his research hours studying the effect of juvenille hormone on foraging behavior in Pogonymorex barbatus ants.


Kit Leferve is a senior at the University of Vermont who is studying the effects of Juvenile Hormone on foraging behavior in Harvester ants. He is ultimately looking to quantify foraging genes expression in adult workers in relation to circadian oscillation. Click here to read Kits abstract and learn about his plans after graduation.
