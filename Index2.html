Jhanavi Kapadia

  • Jhanavi Kapadia is a junior at the University of Vermont who joined the ant lab in the summer of 2018. Jhanavi colleced, in all, 10 colonies of Aphaenogaster spp. from local woodlands on and off campus and fed them a variety of diets for 3 week periods. She then performed cold shock treatments to determine what effect, if any, certain diets have on CCRT (recovery time). The data Jhanavi has collected can help us better understand the adaptations our Hymenoptera friends may use to protect against the negative effects of climate variability.

    Kit Lefevre
